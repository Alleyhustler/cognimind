Heyo! My name is Pan (yes, like the greek god Î Î¬Î½). I'm often called bear or dog, so feel free to use those nicknames as well! I've also been called Mr. Bear, which is adorable.

Everything you see here is made by my own two hands (paws?) unless stated otherwise. I take a long time to draw but soon enough this website will be full of fun stuff!

If you're looking for someone to design and draw simple characters or semi-realistic creatures, you've come to the right place! In addition to regular art commissions involving character drawings, I'm also capable of designing original creature designs for a book description and concept art.

When I'm not drawing or otherwise busy, I'll probably be in some random search to learn more about the world. Whether it's discovering fun facts about colors or linguistics, researching about economy and guerilla, or delving into the intricate social organizations of animals, I'm curious about a wide range of topics. I hope to use this knowledge for creating original works and, of course, to have some fun!

I have very little time to watch series and movies, but I have plenty of breaks and intervals to read comics (manga, manhwa and manhua) or browse on tumblr for new ideas! My list of "want to watch this" or "want to play this" is gigantic, don't even bother, I don't have time to enjoy them but the more time passes there's even more items on that list. I sometimes play videogame but not a whole variety, with little time I like to focus on a small quantity at a time, but I love some casual rhythm games and old-school games! There's always Pokemon Fire Red to play on my phone >:3 I'm now playing Bordelands 3.
