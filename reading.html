The Library!

This page may be not accessible for some users, it uses PDF files and Iframe embeds, which can be hard or innaccessible for screenreaders or keyboard navigating.

For every book you buy and every paper you pay to read, only a portion of the money goes to the actual author, artist, and/or researcher, sometimes even less than half of what you paid for it! (Even if not calculating the price of printing and other materials.)

If you want to really support your favorite authors and science branches, consider instead supporting them directly, some might have their own websites and mail public online.

Some authors might even deliver their book/paper directly to you if you talk to them!

Disclaimer: I'm not responsible for any messages contained in these books and I do not, necessarily, endorse them. Some are there because I like them, some are literally just dictionaries, and some I do not agree with but are there for case study and learning!
